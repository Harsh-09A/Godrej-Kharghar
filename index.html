About Godrej Properties

Godrej Properties Limited (GPL) is the real estate arm of the 127-year-old Godrej Group, one of India's most trusted and diversified conglomerates. Established in 1990 and headquartered in Mumbai, the company has successfully translated its parent group's philosophy of innovation, sustainability, and excellence into the real estate industry, becoming the first Indian real estate developer to receive an ISO certification.

GPL is currently one of India's largest and only truly pan-India developers, with landmark residential, commercial, and township projects in over 15 major cities, including Mumbai, Delhi-NCR, Bengaluru, Pune, and Hyderabad. The company has received over 400 awards for its commitment to cutting-edge design, green building practices, and quality construction, and achieved record pre-sales of over ₹29,444 crore in the financial year 2025, solidifying its market leadership.
